FAQ

What ages are these books for?

Most titles are written for ages 5–10, with simple language and supportive illustrations. Older siblings and parents love the values too!

Are the stories accurate to tradition?

We draw from widely loved puranic and folk retellings while keeping tone respectful and age‑appropriate. When a story is a folk variation, we note that in the book.

Do you ship in bulk?

Yes. We support bulk orders across India and provide GST invoices. Digital PDFs are also available for classroom use.

Can we collaborate for school programs?

Absolutely—storytelling sessions, art activities, and festivals. Write to us with your dates and location.
